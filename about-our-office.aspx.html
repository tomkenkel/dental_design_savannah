  About Our Office

Dental Designs of Savannah is all about you, our guest.  From our convenient location to our practical office hours, we strive to make your visit easy and enjoyable. We are proud to be setting a standard for excellence in dentistry and customer care for the Coastal Empire, here in downtown Savannah.

We are located in the heart of historic Savannah at the corner of Bull St. and Liberty St., adjacent to the DeSoto Hilton. From the moment you arrive here, you will experience only the highest level of service. We offer complimentary garage parking with private elevator access. If you choose not to drive to our office, the DeSoto properties doorman will direct you to our spacious third floor suite.

Once inside, you will appreciate the soothing, comfortable atmosphere we strive to cultivate in our office. From our reception area you can enjoy a beautiful panoramic view of downtown Savannah.

During your appointment, you'll find that our commitment to quality governs every aspect of our work and ensures that we always exceed your expectations. We believe in providing you with technically advanced dentistry and personalized attention. Because we always listen to our guests, we are able to give you the quality of service you deserve and a smile you truly love.

At our office, we believe that we have a purpose: to make an impact in the lives of our families, patients, community, and profession. We strive to live fulfilled, passionate lives in all that we do. At Dental Designs of Savannah, we provide dentistry that changes people's lives. Please contact us today!
