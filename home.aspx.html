  Professional and Caring Dental Solution

 

Dental Designs of Savannah is nestled in the heart of one of the largest historic districts in the United States. A beautiful place to visit any time of year, Savannah is a city rich in culture and history.

In keeping with Savannah's southern charm and old-fashioned hospitality, Dr. Richard Thomas provides patients with excellent dental care in a comfortable and inviting environment.
 
 

Dr. Richard M. Thomas, Jr. is a cosmetic dentist with a passion for helping patients achieve healthy, radiant smiles. He is committed to education, to advances in the field of dentistry, and especially to his patients.
