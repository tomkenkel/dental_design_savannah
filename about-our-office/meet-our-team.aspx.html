  Meet Our Team

Our Team attends a wide range of seminars and conferences to constantly expand our knowledge in Cosmetic Dentistry. Amy and Beatriz greatly enjoy pampering and making our guests feel relaxed and comfortable in our office.

Amy Anderson has been a Dental Hygienist since 1999, all of which have been with Dr. Thomas. Amy is from Augusta, Georgia. She studied at Armstrong Atlantic University where she graduated in 1999 Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Science degree in Dental Hygiene Education. Amy enjoys attending seminars with the office team to expand her knowledge in Cosmetic Dentistry. Outside of her career, Amy treasures spending time with her husband, family and friends.

Beatriz Hunt is a graduate from the University of Zulia in Maracaibo, Venezuela, South America. In 1997 she earned her Doctor of Odontology degree and license as a General Dentist in Venezuela. Prior to that, Beatriz graduated from a music conservatory. Beatriz has worked with Dr. Thomas as a highly qualified Dental Assistant since 1999. She is married and has two wonderful children. Beatriz and her family enjoy cycling, traveling, and many activities with their church, The Sanctuary. Beatriz is our team’s movie critic due to her passion for the cinema.
